My Favourite Hobby
Spelling Bee

I like the Spelling Bee. I like spelling because it challenges your brain to memorize all the words and I really like that. My dream is to get into Scripps National Spelling Bee. I know long hard words such as pneumonoultramicroscopicsilicovolcanoconiosis and Hippopotomonstrosesquippedaliophobia. I know tricky words such as onomatopoeia and kakorrhaphiophobia. In school my favorite subject is Spelling. I even won the Spelling Bee in my school once. This is why I love the Spelling Bee.
